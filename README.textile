h1. Location based livewalpaper for Android

Install the app on your 2.0 Android phone by scanning this QR code:
!http://github.com/downloads/novoda/FlickrLiveWallpaper/flickrlivewallpaper_qrcode.png!

h2. Summary

Flickr LiveWallpaper offers a unique experience anywhere in the world, making urban explorers now Flickr explorers! Your phone backgrounds are uniquely chosen from Flickr depending on your current location, with millions of world locations and Billions of photos on Flickr what will you uncover? 

h2. How to use

In your live wallpaper settings select the flickr live wallpaper and then wait for it to find your location and image.
To see a variety of images, press back in the preview before selecting again.
